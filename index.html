The Cobra Kai Code

Strike First

Always be the first one to strike your opponenet. Those who strike first are often awarded with victory.

Strike Hard

Strike your opponent with purpose and vengence. There is no room for compassion in combat.

No Mercy

Mercy is for the weak and it is not tolerated at Cobra Kai. Cobra Kai is for winners who don't have time for mercy.
